The Patient Panel consistently emphasized the importance of receiving interventions from occupational and/or physical therapists to ensure proper use and their desire for referrals to occupational and physical therapists earlier in the disease course. Early referral to these services can educate individuals with RA as to how to continue interventions independently (e.g., exercise, joint protection, energy conservation, assistive and adaptive devices) to self-manage their disease.
