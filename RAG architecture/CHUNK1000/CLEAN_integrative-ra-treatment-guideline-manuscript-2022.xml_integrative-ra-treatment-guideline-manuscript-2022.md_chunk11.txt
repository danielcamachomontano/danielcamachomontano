Experienced healthcare professional guidance in joint protection techniques at various stages of a patient's experience with RA is vital for this intervention, in order to aid the patient in maintaining physical function. The Voting Panel also stressed the importance of proper patient education in joint protection techniques by occupational or physical therapists. We conditionally recommend use of activity pacing, energy conservation, activity modification, and/or fatigue management over no activity pacing, energy conservation, activity modification, and/or fatigue management. There was no evidence found to support the conditional recommendation that individuals with RA should use activity pacing, energy conservation, activity modification, and/or fatigue management.
