<h1>Table 2. Whether to give or defer non-live attenuated vaccinations in patients taking glucocorticoids, regardless of disease activity</h1>

<p>| |Influenza vaccination|Other non-live attenuated vaccinations|
|---|---|---|
|Prednisone ≤ 10 mg daily<em>|++ Give|++ Give|
|Prednisone &gt; 10 mg and &lt; 20 mg</em>|+ Give|+ Give|
|Prednisone ≥ 20 mg daily<em>|+ Give|+ Defer</em>*|</p>

<p>*Or the equivalent dose of any other glucocorticoid formulation, or the equivalent pediatric dose.</p>

<p>**Defer vaccination until glucocorticoids are tapered to the equivalent of prednisone &lt; 20 mg daily.</p>
