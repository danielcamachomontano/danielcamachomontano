We conditionally recommend consistent engagement in aerobic exercise over no exercise. We recommend consistent engagement in aerobic exercise based on very low to low certainty evidence suggesting that it results in improved physical function but moderate certainty evidence suggesting there is no difference in pain. The recommendation is conditional because of the level of certainty of the evidence and recognizing that patient preferences may vary due to RA disease activity level, the presence of joint damage or deformities, comorbidities, and the cost of, access to, or burden of engaging in consistent aerobic exercise. We conditionally recommend consistent engagement in aquatic exercise over no exercise.
