Similarly, women with APS, thrombotic or otherwise, should be cleared medically by their rheumatologist. The rheumatologist should consult with the reproductive endocrinology and infertility specialist regarding adjustments to the ovarian stimulation protocol in order to minimize the risk to the patient. Women with these underlying conditions who undergo fertility therapy should do so only in centers where the appropriate expertise is readily available.
