This guideline highlights the importance of an interprofessional healthcare team to provide optimal care to individuals with RA. The recommended interventions do not replace DMARD treatments, in accordance with existing ACR pharmacologic treatment guidelines The one strong recommendation was for consistent engagement in exercise.
